The story behind PERO Solutions

Growth in Danish e-commerce has exploded. Likewise, the consumption of disposable packaging, in the form of cardboard boxes and fillings.

A disposable packaging / cardboard box lives, from the time it is folded in the packing unit, until it is discarded by the consumer, on average only 21-24 hours.

It is a huge waste of resources, and often problems arise with the disposal of cardboard by the individual consumer. The waste stations are challenged and the larger production of cardboard - strains the climate.

In Denmark and the rest of the world, demands are made on climate friendliness in all respects, so it is only a matter of time before demands are also made on the packaging part of online shopping.

We, at PERO Solutions, wanted to find a solution to the above problem and have through technological innovation, now developed a product that can reduce the large consumption of disposable packaging in the e-commerce system and the accompanying waste problem among consumers - and which also contributes to an optimization of all links in the e-commerce logistics system.

HOW IT WORKS

PERO’s journey of the packaging

The order is placed

Step-1

End user orders items online and selects PERO as packaging

Webshop

Step-2

Picking, packing, ID scanning and programming of chip

Line Haul

Step-3

Shipments are picked up and driven to the sorting center

Sorting center

Step-4

Shipments are sorted quickly with digital reading

The trucks are packed

Step-5

Using a mobile phone, the operator reads all shipments at once. Packing and route planning is done efficiently

Place of delivery

Step-6

The shipments are delivered and a message is automatically sent to the end user

Pickup

Step-7

End user picks up his goods. Leaves the empty packaging at the place of delivery. Display automatically switches to the return label

Return

Step-8

The returnable packaging is picked up by the operator at the next delivery of new shipments. Return to the depot or webshop

Depot

Step-9

The packaging is cleaned, repaired and stored. Will be sent out to the webshop again

The PERO Product

Packing and sorting shipments is made easier. The chip, which constantly emits a signal, enables the logistics operator to quickly read shipping information, so that a more efficient packing of trucks and a better route planning can take place, - which leads to - space and time savings and less kilometers on the roads.

PERO Packaging 

By using the technological

A recyclable packaging that is so strong that it can circulate a thousand times in the e-commerce system - from webshops with logistics operators to end users and back.

To ensure a circular economy, the packaging is built in recyclable plastic.

To make circulation possible the packaging has a built-in computer chip.
